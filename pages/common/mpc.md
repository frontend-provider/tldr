# mpc

> Music Player Client.
> Program for controlling Music Player Daemon (MPD).

- Toggle between play and pause:

`mpc toggle`

- Stop playing:

`mpc stop`

- Show information about currently playing song:

`mpc status`

- Play next song:

`mpc next`

- Play previous song:

`mpc prev`

- Seek playing song:

`mpc [+-]{{seconds}}`
